case study

nile secure

internal comms comics for nile secure that turned security topics into fast, on-brand stories.

role: illustration + writing + layout tools: illustrator, photoshop, indesign timeline: project engagement deliverable: comic strips + email layouts

brief

make security guidance feel readable, human, and worth opening in a crowded inbox.

build

scripted each scenario, illustrated the panels, and dialed in brand tone across every frame.

result

two finished comic strips packaged for internal email and standalone pdf distribution.

nile secure needed a way to communicate security best practices to employees without it feeling like a compliance checklist. the solution was a series of short comic strips — visual, approachable, and on-brand — delivered through internal newsletters.

each strip distills a complex security topic into a quick, memorable narrative. the visual style matches nile secure's brand while keeping the tone human and slightly playful.

started by scripting the beats for each scenario, then moved into illustration and panel layout. final strips were formatted for both email embed and standalone pdf distribution.
