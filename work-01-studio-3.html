case study

studio 3 concept

concept marketing page designed in figma for a fitness studio brand.

role: visual design + prototyping tools: figma timeline: concept sprint deliverable: responsive landing page design

brief

give a boutique fitness brand a landing page that converts browsers into members.

build

crafted the layout system, wired up an interactive prototype, and polished every screen through dev handoff.

result

a conversion-ready page across breakpoints with clear hierarchy and pricing flow.

a concept marketing page exploring how a boutique fitness brand could present a premium membership launch. the design focuses on clear hierarchy, bold imagery, and conversion-driven layout patterns across desktop and mobile breakpoints.

strong typographic pairings, a tiered pricing layout that guides the eye, and responsive breakpoints that maintain the premium feel at every screen size. every element was designed to feel intentional and conversion-ready.

started with competitive analysis and wireframes, moved through multiple visual directions, and refined into a polished figma prototype with interaction annotations for the development handoff.
