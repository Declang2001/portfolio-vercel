case study

broken hearts

an ai-assisted production pipeline that turns brand assets into product visuals and short-form edits on a repeatable cadence.

role: design + ai-assisted compositing + motion production tools: photoshop, after effects, generative ai timeline: ongoing retainer deliverable: product imagery + short-form edits

brief

turn raw brand assets into listing-ready imagery and short-form edits without rebuilding the process each time.

build

composited layflats and on-model shots with ai-assisted tooling, and templated the pipeline for volume.

result

dozens of polished product shots plus short-form edits shipped across listings and feeds.

broken hearts needed a production pipeline that could turn raw brand assets into polished product imagery and short-form edits. the work spanned layflats, on-model composites, and motion — all composited with ai-assisted tooling and designed to feel cohesive across listings and feeds.

ai-assisted compositing made it possible to produce at volume without sacrificing quality — each product shot and short-form edit stays on-brand whether it's a single flat-lay or a batch of dozens.

built templated workflows in photoshop with ai-assisted compositing for product shots, then extended the system into after effects for motion edits. each deliverable was reviewed against brand guidelines before shipping.
