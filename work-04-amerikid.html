case study

amerikid

designed and shipped the brand's online experience across web, socials, and motion.

role: design + shopify + motion tools: figma, shopify liquid, after effects timeline: concept to launch deliverable: live site + social assets

brief

bring the brand to life online and fuel product drops with real community energy.

build

shaped the identity system, engineered the storefront, and produced launch-ready social and motion assets.

result

a live shopify experience plus drop-ready edits that shipped with every release.

amerikid needed a full digital presence that matched their energy — bold, young, and product-first. the project covered everything from visual identity to a live shopify storefront and social content that could ship with each product drop.

a visual system that translates seamlessly from web to social to motion. every touchpoint — from the shopify pdp to an instagram story — feels unmistakably amerikid.

started with moodboarding and brand direction, built out the design system in figma, then moved into shopify liquid for the storefront. social and motion assets were produced in parallel for launch-day readiness.
