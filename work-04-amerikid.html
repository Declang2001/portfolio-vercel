role: storefront ownership + brand design + motion-led content production tools: shopify cli + liquid, github, terminal, after effects, generative ai timeline: ongoing deliverable: live shopify storefront + motion + short-form edits

brief

bring the brand online and give each drop the site and content it needs.

build

designed the identity, built the shopify storefront, and produced the motion and short-form content behind every drop.

result

a live shopify storefront with motion and short-form content that ships alongside each drop.

amerikid needed a complete digital storefront that could keep up with frequent product drops. the work spans brand identity, a live shopify store built and managed end to end, and the motion and short-form content that ships with each release.

every drop ships with its own product pages, imagery, and short-form content, all following a single visual system. the storefront supports a fast release cycle where new products go live without rebuilding the site each time.

started with brand direction, then built the storefront through shopify cli and liquid with deploys managed through github. motion and short-form content are produced alongside each drop.
